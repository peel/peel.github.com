--- title: "Zen of Refactoring" date: 2015-01-01 layout: post categories: talks tags: jug java talks ---

Software refactoring is hard. We need teachers, good practices, rules. We need peace, focus and ideas. We need the Zen-like rules for keeping our minds in check. The talk maps the Zen practices onto software refactoring using the Red-Green-Refactor and Mikado Method.
